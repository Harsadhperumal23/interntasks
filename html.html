Understanding Servers:

The Backbone of Modern Computing Introduction Servers are the powerhouse of modern computing, enabling everything from browsing the internet to managing business operations. This page will explore what servers are, their various types, and their importance in our digital world.
What is a Server?
A server is a computer system or software application that provides services to other computers, known as clients, over a network. Servers handle a range of tasks, from delivering web pages and managing emails to hosting applications and storing data.

Importance of Servers Servers are crucial in our digital infrastructure.
